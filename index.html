About

NUSE is a voice‑first AI platform that provides financial access in local languages to empower women entrepreneurs. Our mission is to offer access to microloans, business insights, savings, education, and insurance — starting with rural women in the informal economy across Togo and West Africa.

Join us in building financial inclusion and economic empowerment.

Explore More

The Solution

  1. WhatsApp voice note → speech‑to‑text
  2. NLP extracts amount, category, merchant, note
  3. Transaction saved to a secure Sheet
  4. Weekly insights + repayment reminders via WhatsApp

Key Features

  • Local‑language capture for sales, expenses & savings
  • AI insights and micro‑lessons to build financial habits
  • Power BI dashboards for participants and partners

Impact

Access

Lower the barrier to credit assessment through consistent records captured by voice.

Education

Micro‑lessons and nudges to build savings and manage costs.

Outcomes

Track revenue trends, repayment on time, and inventory decisions.

For Partners

We collaborate with MFIs, NGOs, and local associations to run 3‑ to 6‑month pilots. Partners receive dashboards, cohort insights, and lightweight onboarding materials.

  • Co‑design the pilot and curriculum
  • Provide secure data access & training
  • Measure outcomes and publish learnings
