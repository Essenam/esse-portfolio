About

Nuse is an AI powered financial platform that helps women entrepreneurs manage their businesses and build financial confidence. Through voice and picture inputs, women can record their sales and expenses, receive simple analytics to understand profits and costs, and access audio based financial education in local languages.

The platform is designed for women who operate in the informal economy and often lack access to bookkeeping tools or formal financial systems. By combining technology with cultural understanding, Nuse gives every entrepreneur the ability to track income, grow savings, and make informed decisions.

Our mission is to empower women entrepreneurs in emerging markets with accessible financial tools that turn everyday data into opportunity. Our vision is a world where every woman, regardless of literacy level or location, can grow her business, achieve financial independence, and contribute to her community’s development.

The Solution

Nuse combines voice technology and artificial intelligence to make finance simple and personal. Women record their sales and expenses through WhatsApp or a voice interface in their own language. The system automatically translates and categorizes the information into a clear digital record.

Each week, Nuse sends short summaries and personalized tips to help users understand their business trends, plan better and stay consistent. The platform also connects them with microfinance institutions that can review their data and offer fair financial products.

Nuse is more than a bookkeeping tool. It is a trusted companion that listens, translates and empowers.

Key Features

  • Local language capture for sales, expenses and savings
  • AI insights and micro lessons to build financial habits
  • Power BI dashboards for participants and partners

Partners

We work with local organizations, NGOs and microfinance institutions that share our vision of empowering women entrepreneurs. Partners help us design pilots, provide financial education materials and deliver community based support.

By collaborating with Nuse, institutions gain access to real time insights, improved reporting and a deeper understanding of the needs of underserved entrepreneurs. We welcome partnerships that strengthen local ecosystems and bring digital inclusion to every corner of the market.
