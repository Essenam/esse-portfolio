About

Nuse is an AI powered financial platform that helps women entrepreneurs manage their businesses and build financial confidence. Through voice and picture inputs, women can record their sales and expenses, receive simple analytics to understand profits and costs, and access audio based financial education in local languages.

The platform is designed for women who operate in the informal economy and often lack access to bookkeeping tools or formal financial systems. By combining technology with cultural understanding, Nuse gives every entrepreneur the ability to track income, grow savings and make informed decisions.

The Solution

Nuse turns WhatsApp voice notes into a clean digital record. The platform categorizes each message into sales, expenses or savings and sends a short weekly summary with tips that are easy to follow.

  • Voice to text in local languages
  • Automatic categorization and search
  • Weekly summaries and reminders
  • Dashboards for participants and partners

Why it feels friendly

We keep the experience warm and simple. Messages are short, buttons are clear and the visuals are soft. Everything is designed for busy entrepreneurs who are on the go.
